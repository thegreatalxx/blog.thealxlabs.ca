## The Problem with Email Clients

Every email app in 2026 has the same pattern: a list of messages, folders to navigate, buttons to click. AI lives in a sidebar — you click a button, a panel opens, you can ask it to summarize or draft.

That's not Dirac.

## The Vision

> "Email, but the AI is the interface"

In Dirac, you don't navigate an inbox. You talk to your email. The list isn't the UI — conversation is.

```
Conventional: Open app → Scroll list → Click email → Read → Click Reply → Write → Send
Dirac:        Open app → "What needs attention?" → AI ranks and answers → "Send it"
```

## What Dirac Actually Does

### Core Features

1. **Conversational Inbox** — Ask about your email, get ranked answers
2. **Smart Composition** — "Reply to Peter about the UI" → drafts are shown
3. **Semantic Search** — "Emails from Shopify last 3 months" → structured results
4. **Auto-Response** — AI proposes, you approve (no unilateral actions)
5. **Thread Intelligence** — Understands context, not just individual emails

### How It Works

- **Next.js + Prisma + Postgres** — Not self-hosted, Prisma-hosted
- **Semantic Indexing** — Embeddings for natural language queries
- **Privacy-First Architecture** — Minimizes raw content exposure
- **Approval Gates** — Drafts shown before sending, deletions surfaced
